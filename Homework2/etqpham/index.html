These are my 3 data visualizations on the dataset concluding Data Science Salaries from 2020-2023. I chose a bar chart for my first visualization, emphasizing the difference in salaries between each level of experience. I then assembled them in increasing order to show how salary, on average, increases as your experience level increases as well. My second chart, the pie chart, is simple and shows the differnece in average salaries by company size. You can tell that you make the most money, on average, working at a medium-sized company and the least money at a small company, probably referring to start-ups. Lastly, my advanced diagram is a Sankey diagram that shows a flow of change, starting from experience level, then the user chooses a job title that has been summarized in categories of similarity, and then showing how many of those filtered workers work in a small, medium, or large-sized company. This diagram is more of a comparison of ratio or count, as opposed to the other 2 diagrams that show salary based on a factor.
